Why We Like The Garmin Edge 1050 GPS Bike Computer

With the Garmin Edge 1050 GPS Bike Computer guiding us through our training rides, there is truly no limit to what we can achieve. The vivid color touchscreen display makes it easier and more visually appealing than ever to keep up with our training stats, live route details, road hazard alerts, and so much more. We can track our training status, find suggested daily workouts based on our fitness level, and create routes straight from our device to ensure we always know which turn to take. The battery keeps up with our nonstop riding habits, lasting up to 20 hours in demanding use mode or up to 60 hours when we ride in battery-saver mode, allowing us to use every feature to the best of our ability.

Great but BIG

Pros: 1. Screen is a good size for people that want more info or eyes aren't as good as they were. 2. Touch screen is very responsive. I had held back using a touch screen before as I live in Florida and thought my abundant sweat would make them tough to use. Getting the custom short name I wanted to populate on my phone was a pain...and it did require Googling to figure it out. Not really specific to the 1050, but Garmin. Learn more about Edge 1050 GPS Bike Computer

The IPX7 rating indicates that your bike computer can be submerged in water up to 1 meter deep for 30 minutes without damage. This means you can confidently use it in rainy conditions or if it accidentally falls into water, ensuring reliable performance during your rides.

These wireless technologies allow your bike computer to connect seamlessly with other devices. Bluetooth is great for pairing with headphones or smartphones, ANT+ connects fitness sensors like heart rate monitors, and Wi-Fi enables internet connectivity for updates and data sharing. Together, they provide a comprehensive and connected biking experience.

A rechargeable lithium-ion battery allows you to use your bike computer for extended periods without the hassle of replacing batteries. These batteries are efficient and can be recharged multiple times, ensuring you have a reliable power source for all your biking adventures.

A vivid color touchscreen display makes it easy to navigate through menus and options while riding. The bright and clear screen ensures that you can quickly read important information, such as speed and distance, without straining your eyes, enhancing your overall riding experience.

The Edge 1050 is part of Garmin's premium line of bike computers, offering advanced features like GPS navigation, performance tracking, and compatibility with various sensors. It’s designed for serious cyclists who want detailed insights into their rides, making it an excellent choice for both casual and competitive biking.
