Description

This bundle includes a $119 premium TrainingPeaks subscription for just a few dollars more than the item alone. You're getting the best value possible by combining world-class hardware and world-class software to help you make the most of your training.

The Edge 1000 is Garmin's most sophisticated cycling computer ever. At first glance, you will notice the Edge 1000 feature a longer display with higher resolution over the Edge 810 with a slimmer profile. This color touchscreen GPS bike computer puts in-ride challenges and bike-specific navigation at your fingertips. Now you can turn every ride into a race by competing on Garmin Connect segments and seeing real-time results on your Edge.

Garmin has preloaded Cycle Map and round-trip routing so you can explore more, on and off road without the need to purchase maps separately. Connected features include live tracking, incoming call, and text alerts from iPhone 4s or later, social media sharing, weather, sending/receiving courses and wireless uploads to Garmin Connect.

One of the coolest new performance features with analysis is integration with compatible Shimano Di2 electronic shifting systems including all important battery life. Fans of Shimano's discontinued Flight Deck computer will appreciate the customizable gear display with Shimano's accessory (SM-EWW01) that will plug into your existing Di2 Dura-Ace 9070, Ultegra 6870 Di2 and Ultegra 6770 units. The rugged, dual-orientation display (can be used horizontally) is optimized for use with gloves and in the rain.

Key features: Large 3" display, Garmin Connect Segments, Smart notifications, Connected features, Live tracking, Bike specific navigation.

Selected Specs

  • 4.4" x 2.3" Touchscreen
  • High resolution, full color screen
  • Water resistant
  • Bright, sunlight-readable color display
  • Compatible with third-party ANT+ and bluetooth sensors
  • Compatible with Heart rate monitor and speed/cadence sensor sold separately
  • Rechargeable lithium-ion battery with 15 hour battery life
